Our Unique Value Propositions

Creating Mindsets

It’s a combination of technology with entrepreneurial skills through lectures and simulations to create mindsets shifts through a design thinking process. This serves as the scaffolding for the success of startups, sustainability, and scalability in today’s endemic world.

Diverse Learning

The menu options are stackable as needed: pursue CORE + ELECTIVES + ADDED SERVICES. Agility to stack services as per unique needs. It creates a support but diverse community for social and bridging capital that serves individuals venturing into new startups or other employment opportunities.

Practical Learning

Learning in microbursts with immediate applicability. Offered in a hybrid modality to allow entrepreneurs to plug in from anywhere. The limited face to face session allows for community building. Providing end to end solutions for entrepreneurial needs.

Purpose Learning

Using our 30 years of entrepreneurial legacy to back up our proposal and like First Rand, Richfield is purpose driven to provide solutions to our country’s – regions wicked problem of unemployment of youth. By creating new businesses, job creation will follow.

Youth (and graduate) Unemployment in SA


SA context of youth (and graduate) unemployment is a pressure cooker effect and as long as we do not create opportunities for bright but idle minds and hands, social cohesion and national stability is at risk.


According to Statistics SA the current unemployment figure for South Africa stands at 27,6%. One of the best solutions for this is entrepreneurship. However, a worldwide comparison shows that the level of entrepreneurial activity in South Africa is alarmingly low – and for that reason entrepreneurial growth has now become more crucial than ever before.
Essie Bester, June 2019.


Entrepreneurs are not only provided with opportunities that benefit themselves, but their work also develops the economy.


  • Entrepreneurs provide job opportunities.
  • Entrepreneurs increase competition & boost productivity.
  • Entrepreneurs create new business & new markets.
  • Entrepreneurs add national income.
